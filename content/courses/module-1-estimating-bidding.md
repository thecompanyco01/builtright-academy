# Module 1: Profitable Estimating & Bidding

**Course:** BuiltRight Academy — Business Skills for Tradespeople
**Target audience:** Electricians, plumbers, HVAC techs, general contractors (1-20 employees)
**Price point:** $197 (introductory) → $297 (full price)
**Format:** Video lessons + worksheets + templates
**Duration:** ~3 hours of content across 8 lessons

---

## Course Outline

### Lesson 1: Why Most Tradespeople Underprice (15 min)
- The "busy but broke" trap
- Industry stats: average contractor profit margins (5-10%)
- What separates a $60K/year plumber from a $150K/year plumber
- **Mindset shift:** You're not selling labor, you're selling solutions

### Lesson 2: Know Your Numbers — True Cost Calculation (25 min)
- Direct costs breakdown
- Indirect costs most people miss (insurance, truck, tools, licensing, callbacks)
- Overhead allocation per job
- **Worksheet:** "My Real Hourly Cost" calculator
- **Template:** Job cost tracking spreadsheet

### Lesson 3: The Estimating Framework (25 min)
- Material takeoff process (simple method for residential)
- Labor estimation: realistic hours + buffer
- Complexity factors: age of building, access, permits, weather
- Subcontractor costs and markup
- **Template:** Job estimating worksheet

### Lesson 4: Markup & Profit — The Math That Matters (20 min)
- Markup vs. margin (most people confuse these)
- Industry benchmarks by trade
- How to set YOUR markup based on your overhead
- The minimum markup to stay in business
- **Calculator:** Markup/margin tool

### Lesson 5: Pricing Strategies That Win Profitable Jobs (25 min)
- Good/Better/Best (3-tier pricing)
- Flat rate vs. time & materials — decision framework
- Value-based pricing for premium services
- When to charge more (emergency, specialty, peak season)
- How to raise prices without losing customers

### Lesson 6: Writing Proposals That Close (20 min)
- Anatomy of a winning proposal
- Professional presentation = higher perceived value
- Line-item vs. lump sum: when to use each
- Including photos, timelines, and warranties
- **Template:** Professional proposal template (Word/Google Docs)

### Lesson 7: Handling Objections & Closing (15 min)
- "Your price is too high" — 5 responses that work
- "I got a lower quote" — how to respond
- When to negotiate vs. when to walk away
- The follow-up system for undecided prospects
- Red flags: jobs you should decline

### Lesson 8: Tracking & Improving Your Numbers (15 min)
- Job profitability review — did you make what you expected?
- Win rate tracking: how many bids become jobs?
- Adjusting estimates based on real data
- Setting quarterly pricing reviews
- **Template:** Job profitability tracker
