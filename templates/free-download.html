Can I customize the templates with my company name and logo?
Absolutely. Every template has clearly marked fields for your company name, address, phone, email, and logo. Change the colors, add your branding, make it yours.
Do the formulas/calculations work automatically?
Yes. All templates come with pre-built formulas. Enter your numbers and totals, taxes, and margins calculate automatically. No spreadsheet skills needed.

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If the templates don't save you time, email us and you'll get a full refund within 24 hours. We've never had someone ask for one.

That's exactly who we built these for. If you can type in a cell, you can use these templates. No formulas to write, no setup required. Open the file, fill in your info, and you're done.

Yes. One purchase covers unlimited use across all your businesses. Duplicate the templates, customize for each company — no extra charges.

Most free templates are generic office templates. Ours are built specifically for contractors and tradespeople — with trade-specific categories (labor, materials, subcontractors, equipment), markup calculations, and job costing built in.
