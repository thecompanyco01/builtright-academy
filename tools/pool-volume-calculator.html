How to Calculate Pool Volume

Knowing your pool's exact volume is critical for proper chemical dosing, equipment sizing, and water cost estimates. Here's how to calculate it for every pool shape.

Rectangular Pool Formula

Gallons = Length × Width × Average Depth × 7.48

The 7.48 converts cubic feet to gallons (1 cubic foot = 7.48 gallons). Average depth = (shallow end + deep end) ÷ 2.

Circular Pool Formula

Gallons = Diameter × Diameter × Average Depth × 5.9

Oval Pool Formula

Gallons = Long Diameter × Short Diameter × Average Depth × 5.9

Kidney Pool Formula

Gallons = (Width A + Width B) × Length × Average Depth × 3.74
