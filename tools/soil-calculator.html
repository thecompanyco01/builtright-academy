How to Calculate Soil: Complete Guide

Whether you're filling raised beds, leveling a yard, or building a garden, knowing exactly how much soil to order saves time, money, and multiple trips to the landscape supply yard.

The Basic Formula

Cubic Yards = (Length × Width × Depth in feet) ÷ 27

If your depth is in inches, divide by 12 to convert to feet first.
